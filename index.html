The Front Office

About The Founders

Two lifelong Knicks fans who decided the world needed another voice screaming from the Garden. We bring you the stories, analysis, and hot takes that matter.

Andrew Vincenti

Co-Founder & Editor-in-Chief

I'm Andrew Vincenti, a student majoring in political science at Columbia University and I've backed the blue and orange ever since I was a kid cheering in the stands with my dad. I pair my background as a fan with two years of experience in reporting on Ivy League Athletics at one of the biggest student newspapers in the country. My cousin and I co-founded Inside The Garden to build a platform for fans like us to have a voice and foster a community. For me, it truly has always been New York or nowhere.

Hottest Take

"If Haliburton's shot didn't bounce in, the 2025 Knicks would've been hoisting the Larry O'Brien."

Matthew Vincenti

Co-Founder & Head of Content

I'm Matthew Vincenti, a Financial Planning student at Virginia Tech, and I've been a Knicks fan for just about my whole life. My cousin and I started Inside the Garden to create a real place where Knicks fans could interact, connect, and talk about the team we're all passionate about — through every win, loss, trade rumor, and playoff run. As always, Go Knicks.

Our Mission

By Fans, For Fans

Inside the Garden was born out of a simple belief: Knicks fans deserve better coverage. Not the hot-take factory or the doom-and-gloom beat report — real, thoughtful, passionate journalism from people who live and breathe this team. Every article we publish comes from the heart of the Garden.
